SEASONS

A family of former child heroes, now grown apart, must reunite to continue to protect the world.

Season 3 (Spoiler Alert)

Season Three of Netflix's The Umbrella Academy was confirmed on November 10, 2020. It began production in February 2021, and ended in August that same year. The season, which will be released on June 22, 2022, will explore and explain the mystery of the Sparrow Academy, Hotel Oblivion, and the upcoming Kugelblitz caused by The Umbrella Academy that will swallow all of time and reality.

Season 2 (Spoiler Alert)

Episode Title Summary
